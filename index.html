The Birth of CoHeart

Every movement begins with a spark ‚Äî a spark born from struggle, resilience, and the will to transform pain into purpose.

CoHeart was not created out of convenience, but out of a deep and restless yearning: a yearning to inspire lives, to kindle hope where despair exists, to build skills where opportunities are absent, and to uphold dignity where neglect persists.

The story of CoHeart is deeply personal. It was born in the heart of its founder, who once stood at the crossroads of loss and courage. Confronted with challenges of health, limited opportunities, and the weight of silence around him, he learned that life could either end in resignation or begin again with resilience.

Choosing the latter, he transformed his personal battles into a mission ‚Äî to ensure that no one else should feel alone, unseen, or left behind.
