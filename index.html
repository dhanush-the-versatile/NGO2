Our Story

A journey of compassion, resilience, and transformative action

2023 - The Beginning

CoHeart was registered as a collaborative platform bringing together heartful individuals and organizations dedicated to social change.

The Vision Emerges

HerEcho was born from a profound commitment to women's empowerment, recognizing that when women thrive, entire communities flourish.

Building Foundations

Established four core pillars: Girl Child Education, Youth Skilling, Women's Wellness, and Senior Citizen Care.

Today - Growing Impact

Serving thousands across communities, building sustainable models for long-term empowerment and dignity.

Our Philosophy

Dignity for All

Every person deserves to live with respect, autonomy, and self-worth.

Community-Driven Change

Sustainable transformation happens when communities lead their own development.

Compassionate Action

We approach every initiative with empathy, understanding, and heartfelt commitment.

Sustainable Empowerment

Building long-term solutions that create lasting positive change.

Why HerEcho?

HerEcho amplifies the voices of women who have been silenced, marginalized, or overlooked. It creates ripples of change that echo across generations, transforming not just individual lives but entire communities.

We believe in building an inclusive and compassionate society where women and communities thrive with dignity, equal opportunity, and holistic well-being.
